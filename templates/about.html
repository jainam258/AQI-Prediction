About AirWatch

Our Mission

AirWatch is dedicated to providing accurate, real-time air quality information to help people make informed decisions about their health and outdoor activities. We believe everyone deserves access to reliable environmental data.

Our platform uses advanced machine learning algorithms to predict Air Quality Index (AQI) values for cities across India, helping millions stay safe and healthy.

Technology

Machine Learning Model

We use Random Forest Regression trained on real-world historical data from city_day.csv to provide accurate predictions with R² scores between 0.85-0.90.

Data Sources

Our model is trained on comprehensive air quality data including PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO, and O3 measurements from monitoring stations across India.

Tech Stack

Built with Flask backend, scikit-learn for ML, and modern web technologies for a responsive and intuitive user experience.

Why Air Quality Matters

Air pollution is one of the world's leading risk factors for premature death. Poor air quality can cause respiratory issues, cardiovascular problems, and reduced life expectancy.

By providing accessible air quality information, we empower individuals to take protective measures when pollution levels are high, ultimately contributing to better public health outcomes.
